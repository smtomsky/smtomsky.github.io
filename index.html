Stephen Tomsky





I am an Undergraduate of Physics at Benedictine University in Lisle, Illinois. My research is in gravitational lensing, interacting galaxies and galaxy clusters. I am a member of the Large Synoptic Survey Telescope (LSST) Dark Energy Science Collaboration (DESC). I am also interested in the prevention and study of pseudoscience.
