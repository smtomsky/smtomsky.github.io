Students in WiesnerLab at Benedictine University

2017-2018

Christian Estrada, Malik Ata and Stephen Tomsky worked with me on finding merging galaxies in the Sloan Digital Sky Survey Stripe 82 co-add. Malik is now a dental student at Marquette University School of Dentistry.

2018-2019

Zohia Arif and Ahmad Hamed worked with me. Zohia worked on analysis of spectroscopy of white dwarfs, including participating in a night of observing using the Remote Operations Center at Fermilab to operate the SOAR telescope in Chile. Ahmad worked on a project analyzing all known strong gravitational lenses.



2019-2020

Ahmad Hamed, Vanessa Hadweh Smith and Stephen Tomsky worked with me. Ahmad is continuing his study of strong lenses. Vanessa is looking at a relationship between mass to light ratio and morphology for galaxies in the Hyper Suprime Cam 2nd data release. Stephen is looking at improving Python code in the LSST-DESC code pipeline, especially for simulation of lensed host galaxies. Fall 2019
